Diagonal Smile

A diagonal smile is a type of facial expression in which a person uses one side of their mouth more than the other, creating an asymmetrical grin. It is often associated with happiness, amusement, or attraction, but it can also be used to express surprise, surprise, or even pain.

Sad Smile

A sad smile is a combination of a sad expression and a smile. It is often used to convey a message of distress, disappointment, or loss

Natural Smile

A natural smile is a genuine expression of happiness or amusement that comes spontaneously without any conscious effort. It involves the movement of several facial muscles, including the corners of the mouth, eyes, and nose

Happy Smile

A happy smile is a friendly and sincere expression of happiness on the face. It is often accompanied by the wrinkling of the eyes and a slight raise of the cheeks.
